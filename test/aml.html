Anti Money Laundering (AML)

This policy is the Anti Money Laundering (AML) Policy of Giltxchange Global Private Limited (Giltxchange). Any questions relating to this policy should be directed to 419A, Wave Silver, Sector -18, Noida 201301.

The purpose of this AML is to ensure not only that Giltxchange are compliant with current legislation and requirements, but also to show that they take any form of illegitimate use of their services seriously.

Money laundering is where proceeds of crime are disguised so to give the appearance of a legitimate income. Giltxchange will not tolerate any client abusing their services for such matters.

If Giltxchange suspect any client is abusing their services for the purposes of money laundering, they reserve the right to suspend the users account and freeze any funds pending the outcome of an internal investigation. Giltxchange reserve the right to report any suspicious activities to the police or relevant authorities without the client's knowledge.

In accordance with the current industry best practice guidelines, Giltxchange have:

Appointed a Money Laundering Reporting Officer;

Established a risk based approach towards assessing and managing the money laundering risks to the business;

Established and maintain risk based customer due diligence and obtain identification and proof of address in accordance with KYC (know your customer) which includes enhanced due diligence checks for customers which may present a higher risk, this will be determined at the sole discretion of Giltxchange;

Established and maintain risk based systems and procedures to monitor ongoing client activity;

Have procedure for flagging and reporting suspicious activity.

Any client is subject to having their account frozen should Giltxchange be suspicious of fraudulent activity or any activity, which may be related to money laundering.
